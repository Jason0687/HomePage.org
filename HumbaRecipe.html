Humba Recipe

Name Quantity
Pork Belly, cut into 2-inches cubes 1 Kg
Pineapple juice 1 cup
Vinegar 1/2 cup
Soy Sauce 1 cup
Brown Sugar 1/2 cup
Garlic, Minced 1 Head
Onion, Chopped 1
Bay leaves 2
Whole Peppercorns 1 tsp
Vegetable oil 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. In a large pot or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and onion and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add the pork belly cubes and sauté until browned.
  3. Pour in the soy sauce, pineapple juice, vinegar, and brown sugar. Stir to combine.
  4. Add the bay leaves and whole peppercorns. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  5. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let the mixture simmer for 1.5-2 hours or until the pork is tender and the sauce has thickened.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice.
